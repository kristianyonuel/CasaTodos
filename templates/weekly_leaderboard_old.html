üèà Scoring Rules:

Simple Scoring System:

  • 1 point for each game won (including Monday Night Football)
  • No bonus points - just 1 point per correct pick
  • All games are equal - Thursday, Sunday, and Monday games all worth 1 point

üîß Tiebreaker Rules:

When users have the same number of games won, ties are broken using Monday Night Football predictions:

  1. Closest to Home Team Score (either higher or lower)
  2. Closest to Away Team Score
  3. Closest to Total Combined Score
  4. Alphabetical Order (if still tied)

Monday Night predictions are the key tiebreaker when users win the same number of games!
